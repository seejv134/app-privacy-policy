How to Use

Practice on your device. Your settings and usage history stay on-device and remain under your control.

Practice

  1. Open the app and choose Practice (or the relevant entry) from the home screen.
  2. Answer each question in the input area. You can adjust difficulty, question types, and session length in Settings.
  3. After a session, review your results. If the app provides stats or history, you can find them in the corresponding in-app pages.
