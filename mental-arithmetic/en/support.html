Support

If you run into issues while using the app, please check the notes below first.

FAQ

The app won’t launch or crashes
Please update both iOS and the app to the latest version. If the issue persists, email us below with your device model and iOS version.
My history or settings are missing
Data is typically stored on-device. Uninstalling the app may remove this data.
In-app purchases or ads removal issues
Purchases and restorations are handled by your Apple ID and the App Store. Check your Apple ID in Settings or contact Apple Support.
